Welcome to Kids Summer Club!

Our club offers a fun and engaging summer experience for kids aged 6-16. Join us for a summer of exciting activities from Monday to Thursday, 10 AM to 2 PM.

Activities

Arts & Crafts: Unleash your creativity with various arts and craft activities.

Fun Games and Activities: Enjoy a range of fun games and activities.

Sports: Come and join various sports to stay active and healthy.

Role Play: Join us for imaginative play and storytelling

Messy Play: Have fun with messys play activties designed to let you explore and get creative.

Schedule and Activities

Age Group Days Activities
6-8 Monday, Wednesday Arts and Crafts, Fun Games
9-12 Tuesday, Thursday Sports, Role Play
13-16 Monday, Thursday Messy Play, Sports

About Us

The Kids Summer Club is community initiative aim at providing a safe and enjoyable enviroment for kids during the summer holidays. We focus on promoting creativity, physical activity, and social interaction through a variety of engaging activities.
