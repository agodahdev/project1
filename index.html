Welcome to Sheffield Summer Fun CLub!

Our club offers a fun and engaging summer experience for kids aged 6-16. Join us for a summer of exciting activities from Monday to Thursday, 10 AM to 2 PM.

About Us

The Sheffield Summer Club is community initiative aim at providing a safe and enjoyable enviroment for kids during the summer holidays. We focus on fostering creativity, physical activity, and social interaction through a variety of engaging activities.
